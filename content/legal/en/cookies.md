This Cookie Policy explains how Ainevoie uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work properly, remember your preferences and give us a picture of how the Service is used.

## 2. Types of cookies we use

- **Strictly necessary:** required for core features (e.g. security, load balancing, session where applicable). These may not require consent under ePrivacy where strictly necessary.
- **Functional:** remember choices such as language or region.
- **Analytics:** help us understand traffic and improve the Service (often aggregated).
- **Marketing:** used only if you consent, to measure campaigns or personalise ads where integrated.

The specific cookies in use may change as we update the site — check your browser’s cookie storage or our consent tool for current names and durations.

## 3. How to control cookies

- Use our **cookie banner / preference centre** where available.
- Adjust your **browser settings** to block or delete cookies.
- Note that blocking some cookies may affect functionality.

## 4. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by partners (e.g. analytics or embedded content). Their use is governed by their respective policies.
