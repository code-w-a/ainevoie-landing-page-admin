This page summarises how you can exercise your rights under the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and Romanian law in connection with Ainevoie.

It complements, but does not replace, our full Privacy Policy elsewhere on this site — please read both for a complete picture.

## Your main rights

Subject to legal conditions, you may have the right to:

1. **Access** — obtain confirmation whether we process your data and receive a copy.
2. **Rectification** — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
3. **Erasure** — request deletion (“right to be forgotten”) where applicable.
4. **Restriction** — limit how we use your data in certain cases.
5. **Data portability** — receive structured, machine-readable data you provided, where processing is based on consent or contract and automated.
6. **Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests or to direct marketing.
7. **Withdraw consent** — where processing relies on consent, without affecting prior lawful processing.

## How to submit a request

Email **contact@ai-nevoie.ro** (or use the contact form on the website) with:

- your name and contact details;
- a clear description of the request;
- proof of identity if needed to protect your data.

We will respond within **one month** in principle, extendable where permitted by law. Requests are free unless manifestly unfounded or excessive.

## Supervisory authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. In Romania, the **National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP)** — see [www.dataprotection.ro](https://www.dataprotection.ro).

## Controller

The data controller is the entity identified in our legal notice / privacy policy on the website.
