This Privacy Policy explains how AInevoie (“**we**”, “**us**”) collects, uses, stores and protects personal data when you use our website and related services (the “**Service**”), in accordance with the GDPR and applicable Romanian law.

## 1. Data controller

The controller of your personal data is the entity operating AInevoie, as identified in the legal notice / imprint on the website.

## 2. What data we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process:

- **Identity and contact data:** name, email, phone number.
- **Account data:** login identifiers, profile details for customers or providers.

We do not knowingly collect special categories of data unless you voluntarily provide them and we have a lawful basis.

## 3. Purposes and legal bases

We process data to:

- **Provide the Service** (contract / steps prior to contract, Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
- **Operate and secure the Platform** (legitimate interests, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
- **Comply with legal obligations** (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).
- **Send marketing** where you have opted in (consent, Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

We use cookies and similar technologies as described in our Cookie Policy. You can manage preferences via our cookie banner or browser settings where applicable.

We may share data with:

- **Authorities** when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 6. International transfers

If data is transferred outside the EEA, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses) where required.

## 7. Retention

We keep data only for as long as we need it for the purposes described above — including legal, tax and dispute-resolution requirements — after which we delete or anonymise it.
